# Specify

Top-level `Specify:` groups collect numeric-domain metadata items.

```mlg
Specify:
. positive:
  int:
  is: "positive integer"
. negative:
  decimal:
  is: "negative decimal"
Id: "23e4567e-e89b-42d3-a456-426614174000"
```

`Specify:` does not take a bracket heading and must contain at least one nested specify item.
