
# Special Operators

Special operators are not limited to single characters such as `+`, `-`, `*`, `/`, `=`, or `^`.

Any sequence of special-operator characters may be used as an operator, and operators may also use suffixes of the form `<op>_<name>`.

```mlg
*
**
+_-*
^^
*_1
*_free
```

The directed forms `:*`, `*:`, and `:*:` apply to these operators as well.
